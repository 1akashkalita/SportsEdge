## Honest read

The bankroll/metrics machinery exists and the integrity guarantees (METRICS-03) hold. The two biggest real issues for the operator's stated goal ("can I trust whether the model is improving?"):

1. **BANKROLL-02 forward staking** — until a manual `rebuild_bankroll`, daily slips are staked flat 1.0, so confidence-scaling (the whole point of BANKROLL-02) is not live in production. This also means the bankroll number drifts from the intended confidence-weighted basis between rebuilds.

RESULTS-07 and SLIPS-03 are likely *done in substance* (notes record the dry-run passing at 94.6% and 88 slips backfilled) but were never closed out with a VERIFICATION.md — verification debt, not necessarily missing work. Confirm with `/gsd:verify-work 1` and `/gsd:verify-work 2`.
