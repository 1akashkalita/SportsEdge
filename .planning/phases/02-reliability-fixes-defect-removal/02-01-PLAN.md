<objective>
Eliminate the two confirmed Phase-1 failure modes in `sports_system_runner.py`:
the spurious `[Errno 32] Broken pipe` / TASK FAILED alert (FIX-01) and the unbounded
cron-job stalls (FIX-02). Three cohesive edits to one file: (1) sweep all unprotected
top-level stdout prints into the existing `safe_print()`; (2) shorten the Telegram
per-call timeout and add a per-invocation circuit-breaker with a single suppressed-count
run-log line; (3) decouple the per-`log()`-line `obsidian_sync` subprocess into a single
summary sync at task end in `main()`'s `finally` block.

Purpose: These are the dominant, evidence-backed contributors (DIAGNOSIS.md Section 1 +
Section 2 Rank 1, Rank 2, Rank 4). Bounding them satisfies FIX-01 and FIX-02 without
installing the general safety net (RES-01/02/03 — Phase 3).
Output: A modified `sports_system_runner.py` whose stdout writes are pipe-safe, whose
Telegram fanout is bounded, and whose Obsidian sync no longer compounds per log line.
</objective>
